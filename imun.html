IMUN - DIPLOMACY FORUM INDIA

Literary | 30th April - 2nd May International Model United Nations (IMUN) is an academic simulation of the United Nations where the participants play the role as delegates from different countries and attempt to solve real global issues with the policies and perspectives of their assigned country.

Objectives

  • To develop deeper knowledge on current global issues and improve critical thinking.
  • To strengthen participants' diplomacy skills and develop ideas to solve current global problems.
  • To improve researching, public speaking, debating and writing skills in addition to critical thinking, teamwork and leadership abilities.
  • To differentiate between the parliament and the UN, while distinguishing power struggle from diplomacy.
  • To improve international exposure by gaining experiences from delegates across the world.
