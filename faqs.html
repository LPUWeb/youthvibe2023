FAQs

What is Youthvibe?

Youthvibe is an annual cultural and technical fest organized by Lovely Professional University.

When is Youthvibe?

This year the fest will be held from 30th april to 2nd may.

Where is Youthvibe?

Youthvibe is held at Lovely Professional University's campus in Jalandhar, Punjab, India.

Who can participate in Youthvibe?

Youthvibe is open to all students, both from within and outside of Lovely Professional University.

What are the events in Youthvibe?

Youthvibe has a wide range of events, including technical, cultural, and sports events.

How many participants attend Youthvibe?

Youthvibe typically attracts a footfall of over 45,000 participants from across the country.
